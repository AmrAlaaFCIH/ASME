ASME - Cairo University Student Section.

About

ASME Student Section of Cairo University gathers groups of engineering students, who want to develop their skills, prepare for professionalism and step on career life.

Products

ASME SSCU organizes technical seminars, soft skills seminars, field-trips, conferences, orientation sessions in addition to other entertaining events and awareness campaigns.

Our Mission and Vision

To promote and improve the technical knowledge, and professionalism of engineering students. And to prepare a new generation of young professional mechanical engineers or any other related divisions to develop Egyptian Industry.
